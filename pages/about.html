Our Mission

Flood Fuse is dedicated to protecting communities across Sarawak through advanced flood prediction and early warning systems. Our mission is to leverage cutting-edge artificial intelligence and real-time data analysis to provide accurate, timely flood forecasts and empower Sarawakian communities to make informed decisions during critical weather events. We work closely with local authorities and communities to ensure the safety and resilience of our people.

Our Technology

Deep Learning Models

Advanced LSTM and CNN models analyze historical flood data, weather patterns, and terrain information to predict flood risks in Sarawak's diverse landscape

Remote Sensing

Integration with NOAA satellites and DID Telemetry Systems for real-time monitoring of rainfall, river levels, and weather conditions across Sarawak

Hydrological Modeling

Advanced HEC-RAS and MIKE FLOOD models combined with local watershed data for accurate river flow and flood extent predictions

IoT Sensor Network

Extensive network of water level sensors, rain gauges, and weather stations across Sarawak's major river basins and flood-prone areas

GIS Integration

Advanced mapping using ArcGIS and QGIS with high-resolution DEM data for precise flood zone mapping and risk assessment

Alert System

Multi-channel early warning system integrated with Sarawak's emergency services, supporting SMS, mobile apps, and social media alerts

Cloud Computing

AWS and Azure cloud infrastructure for real-time data processing, ensuring 24/7 system availability and rapid alert distribution

Mobile Platform

Cross-platform mobile application providing real-time alerts, flood maps, and emergency guidelines to Sarawak residents
