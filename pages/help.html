Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the flood predictions?

Our flood predictions are based on advanced AI algorithms that analyze multiple data points including rainfall, river levels, soil moisture, and historical data. The system maintains an accuracy rate of approximately 85-90% for predictions within a 24-hour window.

How do I set up alert notifications?

To set up alerts:

  1. Go to the Alerts page
  2. Click on "Manage Notifications"
  3. Choose your preferred alert methods (SMS, email, push notifications)
  4. Set your location and risk threshold
  5. Save your settings

What should I do during a flood warning?

During a flood warning:

  • Stay tuned to emergency broadcasts
  • Follow evacuation orders immediately
  • Move to higher ground if necessary
  • Avoid walking or driving through flood waters
  • Keep emergency contacts handy
